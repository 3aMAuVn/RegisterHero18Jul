Maths Competition Training

Olympiad-style challenges designed to help students enjoy hard problems while developing deep reasoning, creativity, and resilience.

🎯

Best For

  • Students who enjoy puzzles and tricky questions
  • Children preparing for maths competitions or extension programs
  • Confident students who want to deepen reasoning and creativity
  • High-achieving students seeking enrichment beyond school maths
🧠

What Students Do

  • Solve multi-step, reasoning-based problems (not just routine practice)
  • Learn strategies for approaching unfamiliar or “hard” questions
  • Explain solutions clearly and learn from mistakes
  • Practise time management and exam-style problem-solving skills
📘

Program Information

  • Carefully selected Olympiad-style and challenge problems
  • Step-by-step teaching of problem-solving strategies
  • Competitions used as a positive motivator — not a source of pressure
  • Guidance on choosing suitable competitions for each student
  • Support with competition timelines and registration requirements
  • Help planning a balanced mix of schoolwork, tutoring, and competition practice
✨

Benefits

  • Deep logical and mathematical thinking
  • Strong resilience and perseverance
  • Confidence tackling unfamiliar and challenging problems
  • Improved reasoning, creativity, and problem-solving fluency
