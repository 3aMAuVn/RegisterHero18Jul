Group Mathematics Tutorials

Learn maths together in small, supported groups

Group Mathematics Tutorials combine guided instruction with collaborative learning. Students learn together in small, supervised groups while still receiving personalised support.

🎯

Best For

  • Students who enjoy learning with peers in a supportive environment
  • Children who learn better through discussion and shared problem-solving
  • Students who benefit from hearing different methods and approaches
  • Children developing communication skills and confidence speaking in front of others
  • Students building teamwork skills alongside mathematical understanding
🧠

What Students Do

  • Work on carefully designed maths problems individually or in small groups
  • Prepare solutions at home and present their thinking to peers
  • Explain answers and reasoning clearly to the group
  • Practise questioning, listening, and presenting mathematical ideas
  • Learn from others’ mistakes, strategies, and solution methods
  • Solve similar or more advanced problems in new contexts
  • Create their own maths problems with tutor supervision
  • Reflect on feedback and refine solutions with tutor guidance
  • Build confidence presenting ideas in a respectful, supportive environment
🎥

Group Information

  • Small groups organised by year level and learning needs
  • Group size kept small to encourage participation and interaction
  • Focus adjusted for consolidation, extension, or targeted improvement
  • Personalised practice materials provided for each student
  • Tutor-guided discussion and active supervision
  • Sessions offered online or face-to-face, depending on numbers and location
⭐

Benefits

  • Stronger mathematical understanding through discussion and explanation
  • Improved communication and presentation skills
  • Increased confidence working collaboratively with others
  • Greater clarity in expressing mathematical thinking
  • Positive learning habits developed in a supportive group setting
